Hangzhou Electronic Soul Network Technology Co.,Ltd (short for Electronic Soul below) is a young and energetic global internet company engaging in game development and publishing. Many games of Electronic Soul have obtained good results in the global game market.

Electronic Soul has experienced game development teams and global game publishing teams. The entire company has a very nice environment for employees to cooperate with each other and propose new ideas and also profound learning and distinctive opinions about game development. In addition, the company has been taking “forging soul of games” as its duty and working hard to develop and publish first-class games.
